User can enter any word they want , It will check whether the given word is present or not in the dataset.

If the given word is abscent , then it will refine and give some suggestions of words which are nearer to the given word.

If you are ok with the suggestions you can give the review and rating experience .

If you are not ok with the suggestions , it will try to refine again and modify the suggestions .
